Projects

Patient Monitoring Robot

Robot designed to monitor the health of assisted living patients. Follows the patient and detects abnormal motions (such as falling) utilizing an Intel® RealSense™ camera alongside object recognition algorithms (YOLO, MediaPipe). Written in Python and implemented using ROS2 Foxy. I was responsible for implementing patient detection/tracking, patient following, and ROS2 integration and testing of the fall detection algorithms. Team Capstone Project sponsored by Intel.

Awarded the Pioneering Concept Industry Choice Award at Oregon State University’s 2023 Engineering Expo.

AMD/Radeon LOD Tweaker

Program that locates and writes to Windows registry values automatically in order to modify level-of-detail (LOD) in DirectX9.0 and lower games. Only works on AMD GPUs.

🔗 Github Repository

First Robotics Competition (FRC) -- Team A05 Annex #6831

A founding member of our high school's first FRC Robotics team. My contribution highlights include designing and 3D-printing specialty parts using 3D CAD software (Autodesk Fusion 360), developing and maintaining our team's website, logo design, and securing team funding via outreach to local businesses. I competed in the 2018 and 2019 competitions before graduating and pursuing my degree.

FRC Robotics was one of my most enjoyable experiences in high school, and greatly inspired me to pursue my degree in computer science.
